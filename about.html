About The Medicio Hospital

Serving with Heart, Serving with Technology

Hospital Profile

Medicio Hospital was established in 1995 with a high commitment to public health services. With professional medical personnel and complete facilities, we are here to ensure that every patient gets the best and most humane service.

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become the community's primary hospital of choice with superior, affordable, and patient safety-oriented health services.

Mision

  • Providing quality and professional medical services.
  • Developing competent and ethical human resources.
  • Implementing an effective and efficient management system.
  • Innovating in technology-based services.
